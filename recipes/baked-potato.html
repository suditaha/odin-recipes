Baked Potato


Description:

This baked potato has a crisp, golden skin and is light and fluffy on the inside. This recipe is incredibly easy and involves seasoning, baking, and topping the potatoes. The baking time for baked potatoes will depend on the size of the potatoes. It can take anywhere between 50 minutes and 1 ½ hours.

The great thing about baked potatoes is that they're so versatile! This means you can top them with as many toppings as you like — or as few as you like (there's nothing wrong with a plain spud). We love to top our baked potatoes with butter, sour cream, chives, cheese, bacon, and even chili. Let your imagination run wild here!

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) . Scrub the potato, and pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork. Rub the skin with olive oil, then with salt.
  2. Place the potato in the preheated oven, and bake until slightly soft and golden brown, about 90 minutes. Slice the potato down the center, and serve with butter and black pepper. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the top, if desired.
