Grilled Salmon

Description:

This grilled salmon tastes so good! A simple soy sauce and brown sugar marinade, with hints of lemon and garlic, are the perfect salty-sweet complement to rich salmon fillets. Serve with a side of grilled zucchini or grilled corn. This is a great recipe to use when preparing salmon in foil packets. it poaches gently in its on juices and always comes out so tender and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds salmon fillets
  • lemon pepper to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • salt to taste
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup water
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
